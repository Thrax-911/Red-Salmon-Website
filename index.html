About Us

Red Salmon Studio is an indie game development studio focused on creating unique, immersive and memorable gaming experiences. Our goal is to build creative worlds, engaging gameplay mechanics, and artistic storytelling that players around the world can enjoy. We are passionate about experimentation, innovation, and pushing the boundaries of indie game development.
